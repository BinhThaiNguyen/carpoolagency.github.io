Our Approach
We want you to try new things. But we don't want you to give up on what's working. Our approach is strait forward and builds out in stages so not to upset the rhythm of your business.
Environment Design: Show and Tell
First off, show us what you are currently doing - what works and what doesn't. Tell us about your people and your business. Who are your thought leaders, the doers, and the wall flowers? Once we begin to understand how you do what you do, we can start doing what we do. We look at your current technology foundation and study how information flows through your organization. We compare that with what we know [link] and provide suggestions on how we can work together to make work better.

Implementation: Build and Engage
The right tools in the right place at the right time. There is no one-size-fits-all approach to communication management but there are tools and principles that can make the way we communicate easier, faster, and more accurate. Carpool is set up to help build, rebuild, or just renovate your IT infrastructure so your people have the tools that will help them work more effectively.
