Carpool Helps You Do Work Better

We are an internal communications agency designed to help small, mid-sized and large businesses improve the flow of information through environment management, compartmentalization, and working out loud.
We are in love with information. We study the way it flows. We look for where it's born, and where it dies. We find where it lives and where it hides. We watch to see who it impacts and why it sometimes misses its intended target. We analyze who it touches and how it moves. Information is the electricity that gives life to modern companies. At Carpool, we want everyone to get the most out of that life.
Our Approach
We want you to try new things. But we don't want you to give up on what's working. Our approach is strait forward and builds out in stages so we don't upset the rhythm of your business.
Design
We develop communication environments that foster better business. We start by taking a deep dive to learn about your business so we can help you do what you do, only better. This means learning about the people who make up your business: Who are the thought leaders; the go-getters; and the shy, wallflower types? Communication is about people; not technology. That's why we get to know you better so we can build the strategies and toolsets that help you get the most out of your people.
